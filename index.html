About Me

Hi, I'm Fernando, the proud owner of Ferny's Fiesta Desserts. I'm a self-taught home baker with over 25 years of experience creating desserts that bring joy to every occasion. My love for baking began long before it became a business. I used to make desserts for work parties, holiday gatherings, and family celebrations.

But, the moment that truly sparked my journey was when my godson turned five. We bought him a beautiful Toy Story cake from a bakery - it looked amazing, but the flavor just didn't match its appearance. The next year, he wanted a Sonic the Hedgehog cake. That experience inspired me to take a cake decorating course, and from there, my passion grew even stronger.

Now based in Mérida, Yucatán, I decided to turn my lifelong love of baking into my full-time profession. Today, through Ferny's Fiesta Desserts, I create and sell delicious desserts for every occasion - from birthdays to "just because" treats.

Fernando Henry
